Why teams pick Papirfly

Four reasons global brands choose Papirfly over the alternatives

01

Built for the whole team, not just designers

Most DAMs are built for asset managers. Most content tools are built for designers. Papirfly is built for everyone in between — the brand manager, the regional marketer, the franchise owner, the partner agency — because that's who creates most of your content. Templated Content Creation lets non-designers produce studio-quality assets in minutes, with brand rules locked in by default. No design files. No training. No off-brand surprises.

02

Enterprise-ready by default

Global brands trust Papirfly with the content that represents them. Security and compliance are configured in, not bolted on: ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit, SSO via SAML 2.0, MFA, and native directory integration with Entra ID and Okta. GDPR-grade consent management, multi-tenant architecture, role-based permissions, and multi-market governance come as standard.

03

Proven at scale across global brands

More than 1,500 brands and over one million users rely on Papirfly every day — from BMW and PepsiCo to IBM and Rolls-Royce. Brands at this scale don't choose lightly. They choose Papirfly because it handles multi-brand portfolios, multi-region rollouts, and millions of assets without making the work harder for the people doing it.

04

A partner, not just a platform

Software is the easy part. Getting a global brand to actually adopt it is where most projects stall. Papirfly's Professional Services team handles metadata strategy, template builds, migration, and rollout — and stays with customers after go-live. That's why Papirfly is the G2 Best Support Mid-Market leader and consistently ranks as a G2 Leader across multiple categories.
