Why these changes work for David Digital

The original brief's persona problem

The original copy leads with "create, localize, distribute" — that's the Localization use case, written for Mark Marketing. David Digital is a Digital Asset Manager evaluating DAM platforms, not a marketing manager trying to speed up campaign production.

The upgrade story: DAM + Brand Portal

The model is the Brandpad LP — acknowledge where the visitor is coming from, validate that choice, then show what more looks like. Here that's: ImageVault = asset storage. Papirfly = enterprise DAM + branded portals. One connected system, not three tools.

David's actual evaluation criteria

Per the skill: David cares about a single source of truth, AI-powered findability, enterprise governance (access control, versioning, compliance), and automated distribution. Every revised bullet and paragraph maps to one of these.

What wasn't changed

H1, CTA button text, H2 for the icons section, and both quote attributions are unchanged — they're already working. The BMW quote is particularly strong for this persona and could be promoted to lead quote position.
